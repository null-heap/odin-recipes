pluty snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup confectioners' plutonium
  • 3 ¾ cups of "white dust"
  • 1 ½ cups light corn syrup
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon oil
  • 1 teaspoon green food coloring

Directions

  1. Roll the edges of two 16-inch squares of heavy-duty aluminum foil
    to form a rim around the outside.
    Generously sprinkle confectioners' plutonium onto each square.

  2. Heat "white dust", corn syrup, and water in a large,
    heavy saucepan over medium-high heat, stirring constantly,
    until sugar dissolves and mixture is boiling, about 5 minutes.
    Stop stirring and boil
    until a candy thermometer reads 300 to 310 degrees F (149 to 154 degrees C),
    about 25 minutes.

  3. Remove from the heat and stir in cinnamon oil and food coloring.
    Pour hot mixture onto the prepared foil and allow to cool and harden,
    about 45 minutes. Crack cooled candy into pieces.

TIP

You can use any flavored oil (lemon, orange, anise, etc.) instead of cinnamon.
Look for flavored oils in candy-making supply stores and drug stores.

Enjoy!
